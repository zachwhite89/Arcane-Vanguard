Essence & Meaning

Arcane: Mastery of ancient, mysterious, and evolving magical forces.

Vanguard: The leading edge of progress, courage, and transformation.

Together, Arcane Vanguard represents a guild that honors the mystical traditions of Sosaria while forging new paths through magical innovation and exploration.

Guild Lore

In the wake of Mondain’s defeat and the fracturing of Sosaria into countless shards, a convergence of scholars, spellcasters, and visionaries formed the Arcane Vanguard. They were not content to merely preserve the arcane—they sought to transcend it. From the ruins of ancient moongates and the echoes of forgotten runes, they built a new order. One that would not only guard the secrets of magic, but reshape them. The Vanguard walks the edge of the known and the unknowable, wielding spells no tome has yet recorded and forging destinies beyond the stars.

Guild Values

  • Innovation through Tradition: Evolving magic while respecting its roots.
  • Exploration of the Unknown: Delving into forgotten dungeons, planar realms, and experimental spellcraft.
  • Balance of Power and Wisdom: Strength tempered by insight.
  • Guardians of Magical Integrity: Defending Sosaria from corrupted or unstable arcane forces.

Recruitment

Arcane Vanguard welcomes those who seek not only power, but purpose. We are more than a guild—we are a fellowship bound by arcane tradition and a shared vision for Sosaria’s future.

Our Creed: We strive to maintain peace and unity across the shard. Every member is expected to treat others with respect, regardless of faction or background. Our goal is to make Sosaria a better place through cooperation, wisdom, and magical integrity.

Togetherness: We believe in the strength of community. Whether in battle or ritual, every Vanguard stands beside their kin. We grow stronger through shared knowledge, mutual support, and collective action.

Ready to Join? We welcome mages, warriors, tamers, and scholars alike. Apply via Discord or visit our Guild Hall in-game to begin your journey.
